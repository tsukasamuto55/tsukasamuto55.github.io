Our mission

The main purpose of this site is to advocate the local business community to stimulate the local economy. It promotes local businesses by listing brief information about them, displaying their advertisements, contact information, and website links. This site also keeps members informed about upcoming events and programs, as well as helps them strengthen their relationships with others through social media, such as Facebook and Instagram, where members are encouraged to share their stories/businesses.
