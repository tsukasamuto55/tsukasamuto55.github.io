Target Audience

Our target audiences are both male and female who are looking for the information of local weather, news, and events. Their age varies; from teens to in their 70s. They simply like to make a plan in advance so that they could relax right before their plans. They dislike the last minute hassle for any occasion. They also would like to feel that they are a part of a community of Eastern Idaho; hence finding the local news and events is a high priority for them. Some of them are photo enthusiast who love sharing their works and looking at beautiful pictures in general. They need to know about the information of weather and temperature before leaving for their outdoor photo shoot.

Kevin Smith

Occupation: Student
Age: 22
Hobby: Cycling, Hiking, Skiing, Water drafting
Reason for visit: Since he loves outdoor activities, he always wants to know weather forecast for his upcoming outing with his friends and family. He usually makes a plan for everyone, so knowing the weather condition in advance is must. He owns multiple devises, including a laptop, a tablet, and a smartphone. He uses his smartphone the most; hence accessibility of a mobile app is very important to him.

Carolyn Knox

Occupation: Teacher
Age: 35
Hobby: Gardening, Photography
Reason for visit: Carolyn spends most of her free time taking photos of her garden. In order to take great photos, her priority is making her garden look fabulous all the time. She is conscious about the weather so that she could make a plan for various gardening tasks.
