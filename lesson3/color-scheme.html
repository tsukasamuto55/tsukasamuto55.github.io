Color Schemes

My color schemes for this website is mainly Monochromatic Color Schemes, and Analogous Color Schemes for the icon. I wanted to make a clean, organized, and coherent design, and Monochromatic color pallet with blue accomplished what I imagined. I used lighter blue with CSS gradient for background color of body tag to represent sky. Darker blue was used as an accent color for the navbar to make it stand out and let users know this is different from the header. #586ba6 was used for the header and footer because it has good contrast with other two colors. White is used for the texts in the header, the navbar, and the footer, and it represents clouds. Analogous colors for the icon was inspired by the sunrise I saw, and they go really well with blues.
