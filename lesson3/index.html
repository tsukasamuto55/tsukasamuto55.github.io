Site purpose

The purpose of this website is to help people find the information about local weather, news, and events, and to provide them with relaxing moment by showing high quality pictures of various types of weather. The user can use this website to make a plan for an upcoming outside event, to find out about interesting local events and gatherings, and to spoil their eyes with beautiful pictures. It is designed to navigate them to the information they need at ease and bring a sense of community with local focused news and events.
